Our Story

Rooted in Passion,
Refined by Experience

Dandelions & Grace was born from a deep appreciation for the transformative power of flowers. Based in the heart of France, we specialize in creating exceptional floral experiences for discerning couples celebrating their love story.

Our approach is editorial, elegant, and distinctly personal. We work with seasonal blooms, unexpected textures, and a refined color palette to create arrangements that feel both timeless and entirely of the moment.
